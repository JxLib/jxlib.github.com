Recent Photos

These are the 10 most recent photos uploaded to Flickr. The list is regenerated when this tab is displayed or when you click the refresh button.

    Using browsr

    1. Enter some text and click 'Search' or search with no text to get the most recent images uploaded.

    2. Click on a thumbnail to load the actual image in a new tab.

    3. Use the navigation buttons at the bottom to page through the results.
